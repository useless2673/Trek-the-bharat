1

Day 1: Arrival in Goa

Morning: Arrive at Goa’s Dabolim Airport. Transfer to your hotel/resort near the beach.

Afternoon: Enjoy a leisurely lunch at a beach shack. Recommended: Fisherman’s Wharf or Mickey’s Beach Shack.

Evening: Relax at Calangute Beach or Baga Beach. Experience water sports like parasailing, jet skiing, or banana boat rides.

Dinner: Savor Goan cuisine at Vinayak Family Restaurant.

2

Day 2: North Goa Exploration

Morning: Breakfast at the hotel. Visit Anjuna Beach and explore the famous Anjuna Flea Market (Wednesday).

Afternoon: Lunch at Artjuna Café or Cafe Lilliput. Head to Vagator Beach for stunning views from the cliffs.

Evening: Visit Fort Chapora for a sunset view. Relax at Morjim Beach, known for its serene environment.

Dinner: Dine at Thalassa, known for its Greek cuisine.

3

Day 3: Adventure and Relaxation

Morning: Breakfast at the hotel. Spend the day at Candolim Beach for a more relaxed vibe.

Afternoon: Enjoy beach activities like parasailing or windsurfing. Lunch at a beachfront restaurant.

Evening: Head to Sinquerim Beach for a quieter evening. Visit Aguada Fort for historical exploration.

Dinner: Enjoy seafood at Gunpowder.

4

Day 4: South Goa Serenity

Morning: Early breakfast, then check out from the hotel. Drive to South Goa (approx. 1-2 hours).

Afternoon: Visit Palolem Beach or Agonda Beach for relaxation. Try kayaking or just unwind on the beach.

Evening: Visit Butterfly Beach for its secluded beauty (accessible by boat).

Dinner: Dine at The Fisherman’s Wharf in Cavelossim.

5

Day 5: Departure

Morning: Breakfast at the hotel. Take a morning stroll at Colva Beach or enjoy last-minute shopping.

Afternoon: Check out from the hotel. Transfer to Dabolim Airport for departure.

Additional Recommendations:

Travel Tips: Best time to visit Goa: November to February. Dress code: Light, breathable clothing, swimwear, and flip-flops. Don't forget sunscreen, sunglasses, and hats for sun protection.

Activities: Yoga and Wellness: Join a yoga class on the beach. Local Markets: Explore local markets for souvenirs and handicrafts. Nightlife: Experience Goa’s vibrant nightlife at clubs like Titos or Club Cubana.

Cultural Insights: Visit local churches like Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral for historical and architectural appreciation.
